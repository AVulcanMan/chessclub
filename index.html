About Us

About WHS Chess Club

The WHS Chess Club welcomes students of all skill levels who enjoy chess. We meet regularly for friendly matches, strategy practice, and tournaments. Our mission is to promote critical thinking, patience, and sportsmanship while creating a supportive, fun environment where students can grow and enjoy the game together.
